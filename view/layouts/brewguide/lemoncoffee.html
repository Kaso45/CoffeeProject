Lemon Coffee

Description

Lemon coffee is a unique and invigorating beverage that blends the robust bitterness of coffee with the zesty tang of lemon. This unexpected pairing creates a drink that is both refreshing and energizing. The deep, rich flavor of brewed coffee provides a bold base, while the citrusy brightness of lemon juice adds a crisp, tangy edge, creating a balanced yet vibrant taste profile.

Ingredients
  1. 1 cup of freshly brewed coffee (hot or cold, based on your preference)
  2. 1-2 teaspoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice (adjust to taste)
  3. Sugar or sweetener (optional)
  4. Ice cubes (optional, for an iced version)
  5. Lemon slices (for garnish, optional)

Hot Lemon Coffee

  1. Brew a cup of your favorite coffee (black works best).
  2. Add 1-2 teaspoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice to the coffee. Stir well.
  3. Taste the mixture. If the flavor is too tangy or bitter, add sugar or a sweetener to balance it.
  4. Garnish with a lemon slice if desired, and enjoy!

Iced Lemon Coffee

  1. Brew a strong cup of coffee and let it cool.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour the cooled coffee over the ice.
  4. Add 1-2 teaspoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice.
  5. Sweeten to taste if needed and stir well.
  6. Garnish with a lemon slice or zest for a decorative touch, and serve!
