Espresso

Description

Lemon coffee is a unique and invigorating beverage that blends the robust bitterness of coffee with the zesty tang of lemon. This unexpected pairing creates a drink that is both refreshing and energizing. The deep, rich flavor of brewed coffee provides a bold base, while the citrusy brightness of lemon juice adds a crisp, tangy edge, creating a balanced yet vibrant taste profile.

Ingredients & Equipment
  1. Freshly ground coffee (espresso roast preferred)
  2. Espresso machine
  3. Tamper

1. Preheat the Machine

  • Turn on the espresso machine and let it heat up. Preheat the portafilter by running hot water through it.

2. Grind the Coffee

  • Grind beans finely, resembling the texture of powdered sugar.

3. Dose and Tamp

  • Measure 18-20 grams (for a double shot). Place the grounds into the portafilter, then tamp evenly with firm pressure.

4. Brew

  • Lock the portafilter into the machine and start the extraction. Aim for 25-30 seconds for 1-2 ounces (30-60ml) of espresso.

5. Serve

  • Pour the espresso into a preheated cup.
